Canvas

Competitive "paint-to-the-beat" party-rhythm game

Canvas is a party rhythm game where the aim is to coat the most surfaces as possible with your team's paint.

Gameplay

The gameplay of Canvas involves moving your character along the game area to the beat of the backing track. Players leave paint trails behind them and can also shoot on-beat to create clusters or lines of paint.

Whichever colour has the most score at the end of the round wins. This means colours also serve as a basic "team" mechanic, as all players using the same base colour play on the same team.

Soundtrack

The Canvas OST is split into several "batches", relating to the tracks in the game at the time. Currently only the first batch is released.
